The Partnership

Effortless Vacationing Since 1976

Marriott Vacations Worldwide completed its acquisition of ILG, Inc. — the parent company of Interval International — in September 2018 for approximately $4.7 billion, establishing Interval International as a subsidiary of Marriott Vacations Worldwide and strengthening its portfolio of vacation brands including Marriott Vacation Club, Sheraton and Westin.

Elite Escapes chose Interval International as our worldwide exchange and benefits partner based on the level of quality, service and flexibility their membership provides — an integral part of the success of Elite Escapes.

Since 1976, Interval International has been all about effortless vacationing, making it easy for our members to travel to a large array of quality resorts. With hundreds of resorts in more than 80 nations, members truly have the world at their disposal when choosing their next destination.

Each level of membership comes with an array of benefits designed to make Interval exchanges as smooth and hassle-free as possible.
