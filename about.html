About Us

Smiles. You'll See Them Everywhere.

Stroll the 24-acre grounds of Elite Escapes' Patong Bay Hill Resort and you'll understand why — our staff are proud of what they call their office, and what we hope you'll call your home away from home.

Our Story

From a Single, Bold Concept

From a single, bold concept back in 2015, today Elite Escapes is the result of the shared vision of local developers, real estate and hotel specialists alike — a vision of creating a home resort that sets the benchmark for vacation club value and excellence.

Luxurious, modern cottage and suite development — many with personalized pools facing the ocean — that leaves its members proud and eager to return to their home resort year after year.

So come on in, or simply contact us today. From your first greeting of the day, to the service you receive at meal times, to the personalized help from our knowledgeable front desk and concierge staff — come and experience the pride that is at the heart of everything Elite Escapes strives for. It's a level of pride we're sure will leave you smiling as well.

Our Management

A Multi-Cultural Team of Pioneers

At Elite Escapes we employ a multi-cultural management team — a great asset for us, and for you as our guest, providing insight into the many different cultures and expectations our guests have when planning their vacation. From Sales to Human Resources, our management staff bring vast experience in the vacation-rental and lifestyle sector, with the knowledge, leadership and customer-service skills for you to rest assured. They call themselves pioneers — which they truly are, in every department.

Our Core Values

What We Stand For

Quality

We appreciate quality as much as you do. From sheets with high thread counts, to fresh ingredients sourced by our chefs, to the budget we invest in maintaining our accommodation and facilities — you're sure to love the standards Elite Escapes sets and adheres to.

Flexibility

"Different strokes for different folks" — that's why Elite Escapes has built partnerships with travel affiliates Interval International and Holiday Plus. Sharing our standards for high-quality inventory, our partners give guests an unmatched level of travel flexibility.

Service

Any successful business depends on one thing: satisfied customers. We go to great lengths to hire multi-lingual, solution-oriented staff to build and maintain relationships with Elite Escapes members and guests — from pre-trip requests and bookings onward.
