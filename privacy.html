Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elite Escapes Co., Ltd. ("Elite Escapes", "we", "us") collects, uses, discloses and safeguards your personal data when you visit our website or contact us. We are committed to handling your data in accordance with Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA).

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us through our enquiry, booking and membership forms, which may include:

  • Your name and, where applicable, membership number
  • Email address and telephone number
  • The content of your message, booking details or special requests

We do not knowingly collect payment card details or sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and arrange a personal presentation
  • To process booking requests and confirm your stay
  • To provide Member Services and manage your membership
  • To improve our services and communicate relevant information

3. How Your Data Is Handled

Form submissions on this website are delivered to our team by email through a third-party form service (Formspree). Your information is used solely to contact you about your request. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

4. Sharing With Partners

Where you request services that involve our partners — such as worldwide resort exchange through Interval International — we may share the information necessary to fulfil that request. These partners are bound by their own privacy and data-protection obligations.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic cookies and website-analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site and to improve performance. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights Under the PDPA

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal data, to withdraw consent, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
