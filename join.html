Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Elite Escapes membership?
A private members vacation club based at Patong Bay Hill Resort, Phuket — with worldwide exchange through Interval International and access to our Discount Platform of 700,000+ hotels, resorts and cruises.
How do I book my holidays as a member?
Simply contact our Member Services team by phone or email, or use the Book a Stay form. Our multi-lingual staff will handle everything from resort reservations to Interval exchanges.
Can I refer friends and family?
Yes — your referrals receive a seven-day complimentary stay, and if they purchase a membership you receive a US$500 bonus. See the Club Benefits page for full details.
Which resorts can I exchange to?
Through Interval International you have access to hundreds of quality resorts in more than 80 nations — over 3,000 locations worldwide.
